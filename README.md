# GameZone Sales Analysis 2019-2021. 

An analysis for a consumer electronics multinational with multiple product offerings. This analysis tracks sales performance using order-level data, including timestamps, product attributes, marketing channels, and regional information.

With an average order value (AOV) of $281, the company attracts high-intent buyers, making it essential to understand purchase patterns, product attachment behavior, and opportunities to increase lifetime value.

The analysis focuses on uncovering trends, customer insights, and business recommendations that support growth in revenue, margin, and operational efficiency.

Objectives
-Clean and standardize raw sales data using timestamp, product, and marketing fields.
-Identify revenue trends across time, platforms, and regions.
-Evaluate product performance and complementary-item relationships.
-Provide data-driven recommendations to improve cross-sell, bundle strategy, and regional targeting.

Analysis
-Monthly revenue growth
-Purchase platform impact on revenue
Product Analysis
-Top-selling products
-High-margin attachments (e.g., accessories)
Regional Insights
-Region-level spend patterns
-Product preferences by country

Key Insights
-The total sales growth rate is 99.9% increase 
-The Average order value (aov) is 281 indicating a high
-Revenue per visitor is 309
